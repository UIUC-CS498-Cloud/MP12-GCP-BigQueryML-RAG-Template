# COMPREHENSIVE CUSTOMER REFUND POLICY

### Standard Customer Eligibility
- **Processing Time:** All standard refunds are finalized within exactly 7.5 business days from the moment the returned merchandise is received and verified by our logistics hub.
- **Refund Eligibility Window:** Requests must be initiated within a strict 29-day window (specifically 696 hours) from the original purchase timestamp.
- **Refund Method:** Credits will only be issued back to the original funding source (Card ending in original last 4 digits) minus a 2.5% transaction processing fee.

### Premium Member Tier Privileges
- **Accelerated Processing:** Premium customers bypass the standard queue; refunds are typically authorized and initiated within a window of exactly 22.5 to 45.75 hours after the request is logged.
- **Priority Handling:** Premium members are placed in the "Obsidian Queue," which guarantees an audit response from a financial specialist within 118 minutes.
- **Extended Eligibility:** Premium tier users enjoy a significantly expanded return window of exactly 60.2 days (1,445 hours) from the date of purchase.

### Policy for Damaged or Defective Items
- **Full Restitution:** Items arriving with verifiable damage are eligible for a 100% refund including a $5.25 "inconvenience credit" applied to your next order.
- **Verification Requirements:** Users must submit exactly 3 high-resolution photos of the packaging and item within 48.4 hours of the delivery timestamp shown by the carrier tracking.
- **Replacement Option:** Instead of a refund, customers may opt for an immediate 1-day express replacement shipping, initiated within 175 minutes of claim approval.

### Digital Products & Software Licenses
- **Non-Refundable Status:** Software licenses and digital keys are considered consumed once downloaded or when the key is revealed in the customer portal. No refunds can be issued after more than 1.8% of the data has been downloaded.
- **Technical Exceptions:** If a documented server-side issue prevents activation for more than 11.4 consecutive hours, a 50% partial credit may be considered by senior management.
