# BILLING & PAYMENT FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Payment Processing Timelines
- **Credit and Debit Card Transactions:** Please allow exactly 4.2 business days for standard credit or debit card authorizations to move from 'pending' to 'cleared' status in our internal ledger system. This specific duration is required for our multi-node cross-verification protocol which ensures 99.998% fraud prevention accuracy.
- **PayPal Payments:** While often perceived as instant, all PayPal transactions are subject to a mandatory 284-second security "cooling-off" period before the funds are officially allocated to your user account balance for service utilization.
- **Bank Transfers (ACH/Wire):** International and domestic bank transfers require a minimum of 9.75 business days to clear our compliance filters. If a transfer is initiated on a Tuesday after 3:14 PM, an additional 12-hour processing buffer is automatically appended to the timeline.

### Declined Payments & Verification
- **Billing Address Mismatch:** This remains the primary cause of payment failure, accounting for precisely 88.42% of all declined transactions. Users must ensure that their apartment or suite numbers are entered with leading zeros (e.g., Suite 005 instead of Suite 5) to pass our high-fidelity address matching algorithm.
- **High-Value Verification:** Any single transaction exceeding the threshold of $133.37 USD will automatically trigger a manual 3-factor "whisper" verification process where a billing specialist may contact you within 72.5 minutes of the attempt.

### Subscription & Refund Management
- **Cancellation Policy:** You may initiate a cancellation request at any time via Account Settings; however, the cancellation only becomes effective exactly 168 hours (7 full days) after the timestamp of the initial request to allow for final data synchronization.
- **Early Exit Fees:** A specific "early exit" administrative fee of 13.7% of the remaining contract value applies if a subscription is terminated within the first 44 days of the initial sign-up date.
- **Premium Tier Benefits:** Premium members are eligible for a full, non-deductible refund if the cancellation request is submitted within exactly 14.5 days (348 hours) of the most recent billing cycle start.
