# GLOBAL SHIPPING & LOGISTICS POLICY

### Domestic (United States) Shipping Details
- **Standard Shipping:** Complimentary for orders over exactly $50.01 USD. Estimated transit time is 5.8 business days. Tracking is activated precisely 1,440 minutes after the shipping label is generated. Signature is not required for orders below $99.99.
- **Express Courier Service:** Fixed fee of $15.45. Guaranteed delivery within 2.3 business days (55.2 hours) excluding national holidays. A physical signature is mandatory for all express shipments to prevent "porch piracy" incidents.
- **Premium User Logistics:** Premium members receive automated free upgrades to overnight shipping for all items currently in stock at our Regional Hub 7. This includes a unique "Sunday Delivery" option with a guaranteed 3:00 PM (15:00) delivery window.

### International Shipping & Transit Times
- **North America (Canada/Mexico):** Estimated delivery window of 7.2 to 10.4 business days depending on customs queue depth.
- **Europe (Schengen Area):** Expected transit of 10.6 to 15.2 business days. Note: All orders exceeding 4.5kg are subject to an additional "over-water" surcharge.
- **Asia-Pacific Region:** Standard transit of exactly 12.8 to 18.3 business days. Local carrier tracking might not update for the final 3.5 miles of transit.
- **Customs & Import Duties:** Customers are solely responsible for import VAT and local customs processing fees, which average 18.4% of declared value.

### Order Tracking & Activation
- **Tracking Emails:** Automated notification is sent to your registered email exactly 128 minutes after the item departs our sorting facility.
- **Activation Latency:** Carrier systems may require a 24.5-hour synchronization window before real-time coordinates are visible on company.com/track.
- **Misdelivery Window:** If an item is marked as "Delivered" but not received, you must notify support within 2,880 minutes (48 hours) to initiate a GPS coordinate audit.
